🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026 | Last Updated: April 23, 2026

AgriConnect 360 ("Platform", "we", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of farmers, suppliers, brokers, industrial buyers, labour associations, and all users across Andhra Pradesh and India. This policy complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable Indian laws.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, phone number, Aadhaar number (masked), address, district, state
  • Farm Data: Field locations, crop details, soil health records, equipment, livestock
  • Financial Data: Bank account (masked), UPI ID, transaction history, insurance claims
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration, device info
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates (only with explicit consent for weather/field mapping)
  • Photos: Crop disease images, soil photos (only when voluntarily uploaded)

2. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Providing personalized farming advisory and weather alerts
  • Connecting farmers with markets, buyers, and service providers
  • Processing government scheme eligibility and applications
  • AI-powered crop disease detection and yield prediction
  • Financial services: KCC, loans, insurance, subsidy tracking
  • Platform analytics to improve user experience

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with:

  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies on all tables
  • Regular automated backups
  • Data hosted in AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1) region

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell personal data. Data may be shared with:

  • Government agencies for scheme verification (PM-KISAN, PMFBY, YSR Rythu Bharosa)
  • Insurance companies for claim processing (with consent)
  • Banking partners for KCC/loan applications (with consent)
  • Market platforms (e-NAM) for price discovery

5. User Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request complete account deletion
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: File complaints with our Data Protection Officer
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate someone to exercise your rights

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained for 3 years after last activity. Financial records are retained for 7 years as per Indian tax regulations. Users can request early deletion via Settings → Delete Account.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication (session tokens) and localStorage for offline functionality. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

8. Children's Privacy

AgriConnect 360 is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
